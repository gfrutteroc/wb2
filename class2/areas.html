PRACTICE AREAS

Talent Representation

At the forefront of dealmaking across the rapidly changing industry, KLVB capitalizes on its uniquely broad industry knowledge, as well as utilizes its deep relationships with KLVB’s studio and buyer platform clients, to partner with and offer its actor, writer, director and producer clients the highest level of representation.

Television Network / Distribution Platform Representation

KLVB provides business affairs support to both emerging and established television distribution platforms, including traditional broadcast, cable and satellite providers and emerging digital players.

Television Studio Representation

Efficient, specialized and effective, KLVB collaborates with its numerous boutique television studio clients comprehensively across all above-the-line aspects of development, negotiates at the rapidly evolving network level, and seamlessly ushers its clients’ shows into successful production from pre-production through to the wrap of each season. KLVB’s comprehensive studio representation also offers, through its network of affiliates, the highest level of production finance and post production services.

Professional Athlete Representation

Through its relationship with Slam Entertainment, KLBV represents professional athletes in entertainment-related matters.
