Of Counsel

Michael Hoffman is of counsel to KLVB. Mr. Hoffman is Founder and President of SLAM: Sports Law and Management, representing elite professional athletes. SLAM is dedicated to providing each client with the highest level of personal attention and professional commitment ensuring success both on and off the field. SLAM provides big firm experience with a boutique mentality, ensuring that each client receives the best of both worlds.

Mr. Hoffman has over 15 years of experience in representing elite professional athletes, which began at Management Plus Enterprises and later Premier Sports & Entertainment. Mr. Hoffman’s career was founded on working with sports and entertainment icons such as Shaquille O’Neal, NFL Hall of Famers Ronnie Lott and John Randle, and long time NFL Pro Bowlers Willie McGinest, Eric Allen and Ken Norton, Jr.

Mr. Hoffman graduated from Arizona State University Business School with a B.S. in Marketing, and earned his law degree from Southwestern Law School. Mr. Hoffman is a member of the California State Bar, licensed to practice in California.
