Founding Partner

Keith Klevan is a founding partner of KLVB. His practice is focused on the representation of actors, producers, writers and directors in feature film, television and on stage. Keith previously served as outside legal counsel to corporate executives in the advertising, marketing, public relations and branding world, and represented professional athletes in the NFL and NBA in connection with their contract negotiations. He is a graduate of Trinity College (Conn.) and Southwestern University School of Law. Mr. Klevan is a member of the California State Bar, licensed to practice in California.
