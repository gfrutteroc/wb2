PARTNER

Christian Vance is a partner at KLVB. Mr. Vance specializes in negotiating and drafting agreements spanning a wide variety of entertainment matters including content distribution, script development, rights acquisition, above-the-line talent, and product integration.

Before joining KLVB, Mr. Vance held in-house business and legal affairs positions with Newscorp, where he supervised business and legal affairs for several scripted television series and Shine/Endemol, where he managed business and legal affairs for the studiosâ€™ scripted programming division.

Mr. Vance received his Juris Doctor degree from Loyola Law School, Los Angeles. He earned his Bachelor of Arts in English, Literature and Literary History from Georgetown University. While at Georgetown, Mr. Vance studied abroad at Oxford University, concentrating on Shakespearean Literature.

Mr. Vance is a member of the State Bar of California, licensed to practice in California. He has spoken at universities, law schools and business schools on various entertainment related topics.
