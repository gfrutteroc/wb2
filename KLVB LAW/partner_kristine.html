PARTNER

Kristine Blumensaadt is a partner at KLVB, representing actors, directors, writers and producers across film and television. Ms. Blumensaadt also represents television studios regarding all above-the-line matters from development through production. Ms. Blumensaadt began her law career at Stroock & Stroock & Lavan LLP in their Los Angeles entertainment practice. Moving to a more focused entertainment transactional firm thereafter, and then working at both Fox and Lifetime as a business affairs executive, her background spans all facets of industry practice. Ms. Blumensaadt took this experience and co-founded the post production studio, Cinelicious, and soon thereafter its film distribution arm, Cinelicious Pics. After the acquisition of Cinelicious Pics, Ms. Blumensaadt returned to private practice.

BMs. Blumensaadt earned her Juris Doctor degree at Pepperdine School of Law, where she was a member of the moot court team. She is a member of the California State Bar, licensed to practice in California. Prior to obtaining her law degree, Ms. Blumensaadt began her professional career assisting an established television literary agent. Ms. Blumensaadt attended University of Miami on academic scholarship, earning a B.S. in Psychology and Film Theory.
