Talent Representation

At the forefront of dealmaking across the rapidly changing television industry, KLVB capitalizes on its uniquely broad industry knowledge, and utilizes its deep relationships with studio and buyer platform clients to partner with and offer its actor, writer, director and producer clients the highest level of representation.

Television Studio Representation

Efficient, specialized and effective, KLVB collaborates with its numerous television studio clients comprehensively across all above-the-line aspects of development, negotiates at the rapidly evolving network level, and seamlessly ushers its clients’ shows into successful production from pre-production through to the wrap of each season. KLVB’s comprehensive studio representation also offers, through its network of experienced affiliates, the highest level of production finance, production and post production services.

Television Network / Distribution Platform Representation

KLVB provides business affairs support to both emerging and established television distribution platforms, including traditional broadcast, cable and satellite providers and emerging digital players.

Professional Athlete Representation

Through its exclusive collaboration with SLAM: Sports Law and Management, KLVB represents professional athletes in entertainment-related matters.
