CHIEF OPERATING OFFICER

Diana Longarzo is the chief operating officer of KLVB, overseeing all aspects of the firmâ€™s non law-related business, including firm strategy, finance, accounting and office management.

Ms. Longarzo is also President, JLD Media, Inc., a firm providing a wide range of business consulting services, including television production services, business strategy, M&A advisory, and digital product development across a variety of entertainment-related industries.

A 20-year veteran of The Walt Disney Company, Ms. Longarzo held several senior management positions there, including Vice President Strategy and Business Development for Disney Interactive; Vice President Special Markets for Disney Channel; and Senior Vice President for Disney Televentures, a video programming partnership among five regional telephone companies and Disney. Prior to her work at Disney, Ms. Longarzo held posts at Twentieth Century Fox Television and the management consulting firm Bain & Co.

Ms. Longarzo earned a Masters of Business Administration from Stanford University and a Bachelor of Arts degree in Mathematical Economics from Pomona College. Ms. Longarzo currently serves on the Parents Advisory Council of Georgetown University McDonough School of Business.
